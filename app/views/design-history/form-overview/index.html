Form States

Forms can be in one of three states:

Draft

A form that's being worked on but isn't live yet.

  • Created and updated dates
  • Preview link available

Live

A form that's published and available to users.

  • Live date
  • Preview and live links available

Live with Draft

A form that's live but has a new version being worked on.

  • Shows both live and draft information
  • All links available for both versions
